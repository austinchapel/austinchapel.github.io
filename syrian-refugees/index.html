The Syrian Civil War that began in 2011 has put 13.5 million in need of help. They are fleeing brutal violence and the collapes of their home country's infrastructure.

The Facts

  • The conflict has created 4.8 million refugees, half of which are children.
  • Beyond that, there are 6.6 million people displaced from their homes within Syria.
  • As of February 26th, there is a partial ceasefire providing the opportunity for humanitarian aid to people cut off for months.

Austin Chapel is a church plant in central Austin striving for a Jesus-centered community of grace and love.

Our purpose is God's glory, nothing more and nothing less. Our mission is to make disciples of Jesus.

We are committed to serving Jesus. We find our identity and value in Him, and we cannot help but share the difference this has made in our lives.

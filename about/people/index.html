Steven Montgomery is the lead pastor of Austin Chapel. He and his wife Melissa met at Bible college. They have been married for four years and have a son named Lincoln. Steven studied theology at Calvary Chapel Bible College. He has always felt called to plant a church, and he has helped three other churches get started here in Austin.

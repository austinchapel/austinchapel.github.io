Mission

We partner together in this vision in Worship, Community, Service, and Mission.

Glory of God

We do all things to the glory of God. We offer our lives as spiritual sacrifices. We live for His glory, exalting Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Christ Centered Community

We strive for true Christ centered community spurring one another on towards the gospel and serving our King. Bearing with each others weaknesses and building one another up in love.

Make Disciples

Our mission is to build God’s Kingdom, we do this by making disciples and preaching the gospel! The gospel has been carried one generation to the next by the sharing of the gospel and investing in people’s lives by making disciples. One person at a time one relationship at a time we will see revival in this city, nation and world!
